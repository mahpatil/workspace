# LinkedIn Post Style Guide

## Voice & Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Personal stories with business insights
- Conversational, not corporate-speak
- Authentic and reflective

## Structure
- **Hook**: First 2 lines must grab attention (question, bold statement, or contrast)
- **Line breaks**: Generous use of line breaks for readability (every 2-3 sentences)
- **Story/Insight**: Share personal experience or observation
- **Lesson/Takeaway**: Clear value for the reader
- **Call to engage**: End with a question or invitation to comment

## Hashtags
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Mix of broad (#Leadership) and specific (#GenZ #FutureOfWork)
- Place at the end

## Content Categories

### Leadership
- Management lessons learned
- Team building insights
- Challenging conventional wisdom
- Mentorship stories

### Career
- Career navigation advice
- Skill development
- Industry observations
- Personal growth reflections

## Engagement Principles
- Share vulnerabilities, not just wins
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Respond to comments
- Build community, not just broadcast
